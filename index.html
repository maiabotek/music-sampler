Whether or not you realize it, there's a good chance you've probably heard a song by Aubrey Graham at some point in your life. Known by his stage name, Drake, the Canadian actor, entrepreneur and artist has dominated charts across multiple genres including hip-hop, R&B and pop. He's released over 140 singles, eight studio albums and numerous mixtapes, which have won him four Grammy Awards and 47 nominations. No matter your preference or taste in music, Drake offers songs to dance to, cry to or just relax to. His lyrics are often witty, though sometimes emotional, and the beats that accompany his vocals elevate his songs from other artists in the hip-hop genre.

Her Loss is Drake's most recent studio album, a collaboration with artist 21 Savage that was released Nov. 4. The following song is titled "Jumbotron S**t Poppin."

Honestly Nevermind was released earlier in 2022. The following song is titled "Sticky," and features a more techno and upbeat rhythm than Drake's other songs.

Certified Lover Boy was released in 2021. This song is titled "Race my Mind".

More Life is a mixtape-style project that Drake released in 2017. This song is titled "Passionfruit."
